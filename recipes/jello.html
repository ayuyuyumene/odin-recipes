Coffee Jelly

Description

Coffee jelly is quite simple to make and there are many different ways of serving it. One common way of serving it is to make the jelly in a glass and top with cream once the jelly is set

Ingredients

  • 6g gelatine powder
  • 60ml cold water
  • 250ml strong hot coffee
  • 150ml cream
  • 10g sugar

Steps

  1. Add the cold water to a small container and sprinkle the gelatine and soak in it for about 5 minutes until the gelatine becomes soft and spongy.
  2. Add the gelatine to the hot coffee and mix well ensuring that gelatine is dissolved and there are no gelatine lumps.
  3. Pour the coffee mixture into 2 or 3 short glasses and chill them in the fridge for minimum 3 hours.
  4. While waiting the jelly is in the fridge to set, add cream and sugar in a bowl.
  5. Whip the cream until it thickens only slightly to a consistency a bit thinner than cream soup.
  6. When the coffee jelly is set, pour sweet thickened cream over the jelly, decorate with your choice of decoration if using.

Strawberry shortcake

Description

This homemade summer dessert idea has a perfect vanilla cake layered with fresh whipped cream and strawberries.

Ingredients

  • 450g strawberries
  • 100g sugar
  • 125g all-purpose flour
  • 4 eggs
  • 60g butter
  • 300ml whipped cream
  • Syrup (optional)

Steps

  1. Add â…” of the sugar to the egg yolk in a bowl and beat the egg yolk until it becomes whitish and thick
  2. Beat the egg whites in a different bowl with the remaining sugar to make meringue
  3. Add the flour to the beaten egg yolk and mix to make smooth batter.
  4. Fold the batter into the meringue.
  5. Pour the batter into a cake pan and cook in the oven.(180 degrees C)
  6. Cool the sponge cake upside down so that the top of the cake is perfectly flat and easy to decorate.
  7. Slice the cake horizontally in the middle to make two round cake pieces. Coat the cut side of the cake with syrup.
  8. Spread the bottom part of the cake with the whipped cream, then place halved strawberries on top, covering the entire surface.
  9. Cover the strawberries with more cream, then put the top part of the sponge cake on it.
  10. Coat the side and the top of the cake with cream, then decorate the outer edge of the round surface using a piping tip.
  11. Place the strawberries on top of the cake.

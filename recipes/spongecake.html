Sponge cheseecake

Description

Its light, fluffy, moist and less sweet than any other cheesecakes.

Ingredients

  • 250g cream chesee
  • 60g butter
  • 2 tablespoons milk
  • 3 eggs

Steps

  1. The meringue needs to have a soft peak. Do not beat the meringue too much and form a hard peak. When you make a peak in the bowl by lifting the whisker, the peak should slightly bend.
  2. Bake at low temperature for a long time. Initially bake at 160C (320F) for 20 minutes, then at 140C (284F) for 40 minutes.
  3. Bake in a water bath. When baking for a long time, the cake tends to dry up. The water bath keeps the cake moist.
  4. Lay an extra baking pan underneath the water bath. Along with the 2nd point above, this is also a tip to prevent cracking the surface.
  5. After turning off the oven, leave the cake in for 30 minutes, then gradually cool down with the oven door open for another 20 minutes.
